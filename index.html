The Artist

Who
We Are

BLKMTH is an independent tattoo practice based in Bali, Indonesia. Since 2017, we have been creating bold, lasting work — driven by personal stories, clean linework, and a deep respect for the craft.

Every tattoo is a collaboration. Bring your idea, your reference, or nothing at all — and we'll build something worth wearing forever.
